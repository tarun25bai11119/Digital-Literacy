Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide


Part B – Prevention Checklist


Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips


* General Safety Tips
1. Never share OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone.
2. Never click on unknown links sent through SMS or WhatsApp.
3. Always verify the caller before trusting any banking related call.
4. Use strong passwords combining letters and numbers.
5. Enable two-step verification in important apps.
6. Keep your mobile apps updated regularly.
7. Do not save banking details on unknown websites.
8. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.




* UPI / Financial Safety Tips
9. Always remember UPI PIN is only required to send money, not to receive money.
10. Never approve unknown payment collect requests.
11. Always check the receiver name before sending money.
12. Immediately block your bank services if fraud is suspected.




* Reporting Cybercrime
13. Report cyber fraud on National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
14. Call Cyber Crime Helpline Number: 1930 for immediate help in financial fraud cases.
